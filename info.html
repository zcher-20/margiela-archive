This is a visual archive inspired by the Maison Margiela Folders, a personal project built by Zayneb Cherif.

The Folders are Maison Margiela's living reference system: binders of research, clippings, and images that have guided the house's creative process since its founding. They span themes central to the Margiela vocabulary, including Artisanal, Anonymity, Tabi, and Bianchetto, each a lens into the philosophy and craft behind the collections.

This archive gathers printed research, digital imagery, and shipping documentation from across the Folders, organized as a browsable index. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Maison Margiela. It is an independent study of the material, built out of admiration for the house and its methods.
